This is currently a work-in-progress project that I am working on for my senior design class. I will update it as soon as the final project is complete (May 2025 is when this project will be complete).

For now, enjoy this presentation. I had to present it with my groupmates at the end of the first semester of the class, and it represents some of our initial goals for the project and what research we've done. The second semester of the class is focused on building and implementing the robot itself.
